Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

POPIA Compliance Notice: VettedStay is fully compliant with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly and transparently.

1. Introduction

VettedStay (Pty) Ltd ("we", "us", or "our") operates tenant screening services in South Africa. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use our tenant screening services, we collect:

  • Full name and contact details (email, phone number)
  • South African ID number
  • Residential address
  • Employment information
  • Rental history
  • Consent documentation

2.2 Information From Third Parties

To complete background checks, we obtain information from:

  • Credit bureaus (TransUnion, Experian, CompuScan)
  • Department of Home Affairs
  • Court records databases
  • Previous landlords and employers (with consent)

2.3 Automated Information

We collect technical information including:

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Usage data and analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Process tenant screening requests
  • Generate comprehensive background reports
  • Verify identity and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Improve our services
  • Communicate with you about your order
  • Send important service updates

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under POPIA, we process your personal information based on:

  • Consent: You provide explicit consent for background screening
  • Contractual necessity: Required to fulfill our service agreement
  • Legal obligation: Compliance with South African laws
  • Legitimate interests: Fraud prevention and service improvement

5. Consent Requirements

Important: All tenant screenings require explicit written consent from the applicant. Landlords must obtain and provide proof of consent before ordering any screening report.

Your consent includes:

  • Authorization to collect personal information
  • Permission to access credit bureau records
  • Consent to contact previous landlords and employers
  • Agreement to share the report with the requesting landlord

6. Information Sharing and Disclosure

6.1 Who We Share With

We share personal information only with:

  • Landlords: Who commissioned the screening (with your consent)
  • Credit bureaus: For credit history verification
  • Government agencies: For identity and records verification
  • Service providers: IT infrastructure and payment processing (under strict confidentiality)

6.2 We Never Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Employee confidentiality agreements
  • Multi-factor authentication for system access
  • Encrypted backups

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information for:

  • Screening reports: 5 years (legal requirement)
  • Transaction records: 7 years (tax compliance)
  • Consent documentation: Duration of retention + 1 year
  • Marketing preferences: Until you unsubscribe

After retention periods expire, we securely delete or anonymize all personal information.

9. Your Rights Under POPIA

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal information
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Object: Object to processing for legitimate interests
  • Restrict: Request restriction of processing
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent at any time
  • Complain: Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator

  • Improve user experience

11. Third-Party Services

13. International Transfers

Your personal information is primarily stored and processed in South Africa. If we transfer data internationally, we ensure adequate protection through:

  • Adequacy decisions
  • Binding corporate rules
